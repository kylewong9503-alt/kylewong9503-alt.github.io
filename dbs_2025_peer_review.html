What are the future drivers
How the market
looks like
Under low-rate environment that NII is under pressure, leading peers are relying more on non-NII (particularly wealth management) to grow revenue
Corporate loan demand remained stagnant and peers are cautious in consumer finance
Ample funding from wealth management is deployed into bond investment (mainly Govie. and policy bank bond)
Tightly managing expenses and headcount. NPLs remained stable and provisions kept at an adequate level
What we would do
next year
Continue to grow low-cost deposits and loans at reasonable margin
Nimbly deploy surplus funding to maximize NII
Accelerate Non-NII growth by turbocharging wealth management and capturing two-way investment opportunities of FIs
Continue to drive productivity and explore use of AI
Prudent credit risk management and minimize impairment
